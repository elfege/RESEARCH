## 2. Writing Standards

### 2.1 Style

- **2.1.1** Bilingual when requested (French primary, English translation)
- **2.1.2** Short, propositional form preferred over long treatises
- **2.1.3** Cite Hegel by work and section (e.g., *Science de la Logique*, Doctrine de l'Essence)
- **2.1.4** No false modesty, no inflation — state contributions plainly

### 2.2 Philosophical Rigor

- **2.2.1** Distinguish Phénoménologie (1807) from Science de la Logique (1812-1816) contexts
- **2.2.2** für sich = structure logique pure (Logique), not necessarily consciousness (Phéno)
- **2.2.3** User's PhD: "Une critique hégélienne de Hegel" — quantity category in the Science of Logic
- **2.2.4** PhD is in Philosophy (Logic & Epistemology), NOT formal/mathematical logic
